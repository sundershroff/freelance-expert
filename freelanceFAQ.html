Hiring Managaer

1. What is the role of a Hiring Manager?

A Hiring Manager at MARRIMMA Ai-TechLabs India 1 (OPC) Private Limited is responsible for overseeing the recruitment and verification of freelancers. This includes conducting interviews, verifying documents, and ensuring that all hired freelancers comply with company policies and standards.

2. How do I become a Hiring Manager?

To become a Hiring Manager, you need to sign up on our website/App and complete the registration form with your personal, educational, and professional details. After submitting the form and paying the verification fees, your information will be reviewed, and upon approval, you will be appointed as a Hiring Manager.

3. What documents are required for the registration process?

The registration process requires you to provide:

  • Government-issued photo ID
  • Aadhaar/PAN/SSN Number
  • Driving License (if applicable)
  • Educational certificates
  • Work experience documents (if applicable)
  • Notary License (if applicable)

4. Is there a fee for the verification process?

Yes, there is a verification fee that must be paid during the registration process. This fee covers the cost of verifying your documents and credentials.

5. What are the responsibilities of a Hiring Manager?

A Hiring Manager's responsibilities include:

  • Verifying the original certificates and identification of freelancers
  • Uploading verified documents into the system
  • Capturing and uploading photos of freelancers and themselves
  • Ensuring all verification statements are accurate
  • Releasing commissions to freelancers upon successful completion of work

6. What happens if there are discrepancies in the provided information?

If discrepancies are found in the provided information, the application may be delayed or rejected. It is crucial to ensure that all submitted details and documents are accurate and up-to-date.

7. Can I apply if I have a criminal record?

Yes, you can apply if you have a criminal record. However, you must provide a detailed explanation of the circumstances. Each case is reviewed individually to determine eligibility.

8. What is the role of the Senior Hiring Manager?

The Senior Hiring Manager oversees the hiring process, monitors the activities of Hiring Managers, and ensures the smooth operation of the recruitment system. They also handle the release of commissions and provide feedback on the hiring process.

9. How do I track the status of my application?

After submitting your application, you can track its status through the dashboard on our website/App. You will receive notifications regarding the progress and any actions needed from your end.

10. What is the feedback system for Hiring Managers?

The feedback system collects input from Profile Finders and other freelancers regarding the performance of Hiring Managers. This feedback is used for internal assessment and improvement purposes and is not publicly displayed. Consistently negative feedback may result in termination.

11. Can I refer a friend to become a Hiring Manager?

Yes, you can refer a friend to become a Hiring Manager. They will need to go through the same registration and verification process to be considered for the role

12. What are the benefits of being a Hiring Manager?

Benefits of being a Hiring Manager include a role in shaping the recruitment process, opportunities for professional growth, and the potential for earning commissions through successful freelancer hires.

13. How do I contact support for help with my application?

For any assistance with your application, you can contact our support team through the Help section on our website/App. You can also reach us via email or phone for any specific queries.

Profile Manager

1. What is the role of a Profile Manager?

A Profile Manager is responsible for creating, verifying, and managing profiles of Profile Finders. They ensure all information is accurate and complete, and they handle various profile-related tasks.

2. How do I register as a Profile Manager?

You can register by signing up on our website or app. You will need to provide your personal information, verify your email and mobile number, and agree to our Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy.

3. What documents are required for registration?

You will need to provide your first and last name, email ID, mobile number, date of birth, address, and government-issued photo ID. You will also need to submit educational qualifications and work experience details.

4. What are the subscription plans available for Profile Managers?

We offer various subscription plans, including Base, Middle, Upper Middle, Rich, Super Rich, and Royal. Each plan provides different benefits and commission rates based on the level of service and experience.

5. How is the verification process conducted?

The verification process involves verifying original certificates and identification, uploading these documents into the system, and capturing photos of the Profile Finders and yourself for authentication.

6. What are the responsibilities of a Profile Manager??

Profile Managers are responsible for verifying profile information, handling profile-related tasks, and providing support to Profile Finders. They ensure that all information is accurate and complete and manage the profile verification process.

7. How can I contact a Hiring Manager for further assistance?

After successful registration, you can select a Hiring Manager from your dashboard. Contact details of the Hiring Manager will be provided based on your location.

8. What happens if I need to update my profile after approval?

Any changes or updates to your profile after approval will require re-verification by a Hiring Manager. You will need to follow the verification process again to ensure all information is up to date.

9. What is the additional paid job option for Profile Managers?

Profile Managers have the option for an additional paid job from Profile Finders for profile handling. This is an optional service that Profile Finders can choose in the add-on section

10. What support is available if I need help with my role?

Support is available through our Help section on the website/App. You can contact our support team via email or whatsapp for any specific queries or assistance.
